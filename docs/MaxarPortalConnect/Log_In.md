# Log In
To use the plugin you must log in by providing your Maxar Portal Services credentials.

1. Fill in the username and password fields.
2. Click Log In
3. If you are logged in successfully, you will see a "Logged in Successfully" message and the Log In button will be grayed out.

** Notes **

* If you want to change your Log In session, click Log Out.
* If you see a 401 error, you have entered incorrect credentials.
* If you see a 403 error, there is a problem with your user or your organization's account.
